## Elastomers
A flexible chain with $n$ monomers of size $a$ has:
- a radius of gyration (mean end-to-end distance) $r_{g}=\sqrt{n}a$
- an entropic spring constant of

$$k=\frac{k_{B}T}{r_{g}^{2}}=\frac{k_{B}T}{na^{2}}$$
